About Catharsis Reborn

Catharsis Reborn is an total conversion mod of Postal III, and aims to right everything trashmasters did wrong, and even making the game better than it would have been had everything went right with the development of Postal 3 in the first place! Essentially cleaning trashmasters dirty diapers after they crapped this terrible game out, CR will contain a new fleshed out story, a free-roaming world, more neat weapons to play around with (and some various tweaks to the other weapons), a new original soundtrack, and way too many new mechanics, features, optimizations, gameplay tweaks, and bug fixes to name!
