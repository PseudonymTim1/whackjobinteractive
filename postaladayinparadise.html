About Postal: A Day In Paradise

Postal: A Day In Paradise is a mod for the original 1997 POSTAL game developed by WJI members Elwood and Kenny_MCII, released in November 2017 to celebrate the 20th anniversary of the series. It keeps the original POSTAL 1 maps (as there are no tools to make new ones) but changes enemy and item placements, along with the order and gameplay of the levels. The mod also features a new storyline, new soundtrack, new selection of Dude lines and new sound effects.
