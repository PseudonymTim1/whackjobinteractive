About our team

Whackjob Interactive was founded around November of 2015, and started out with the development of our first and most ambitious project, (Catharsis Reborn) and a very terrible and now abandoned discord server. We have came a long way since our humble beginnings! Our team of indie developers consist of all types of experianced modders, programmers, writers, game developers, etc, from all across the world! Our team is solely dedicated to providing more Postal content to the Postal fans all over the world!
