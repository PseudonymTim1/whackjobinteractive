About us

Welcome to the Whackjob Interactive official website! We are a "talented" team of indie developers working on various projects from all across the globe, but mainly from Europe. We currently make mods for various video games in the spirit of the postal game franchise. Providing die-hard Postal fans with high quality Postal content. We put our heart and soul into the development of our mods and we don't regret anything!

Our Mods

Not suprisingly, yes, we make mods! And here's a list of all of them, labled as in development, development haulted, or not in-development. If a mod is listed as "Completed!" then it is available for download from our moddb, link is located in the "social media" section of this page

Catharsis Reborn

 

- IN ACTIVE DEVELOPMENT! -
A total conversion mod for Postal III, essentially changing Trashmaster's dirty diapers, Catharsis Reborn is a complete overhaul. New story, a ton of fixes, and too many other new features to name!

Learn More...

Postal: A Day In Paradise

 

- COMPLETED! -
Postal: A Day In Paradise is a mod for the original 1997 POSTAL game made for fun by just three Whackjob developers back in 2016, adding a new campaign level and some other neat stuff

Learn More...

Postal: Share The Doom

 

- IN ACTIVE DEVELOPMENT! -
Using 3D models exported from Postal 2, along with sounds, and textures. The aim of the mod is to try to recreate Postal 2 into GZDoom as close as possible! (Note: The name of the mod isn't permanent, and it may change in the future)
